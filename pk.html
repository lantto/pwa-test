Player Killing

Legal statuses

  • Attacking pets or players will flag you a criminal (grey)
  • Killing players will flag you a murderer (red)
  • Claiming the throne will flag you a criminal but anyone attacking you while you're claiming will also be flagged a criminal (including the King)
  • Criminal status lasts for 10 minutes
  • Murderer status lasts for 1 hour but is reset every time you kill someone and another 10 minutes are added for each kill during the flag period

Disadvantages

  • You will not be able to log out until your flag wears off
  • People will be able to attack you in protected zones (towns)
  • People will see you on the map and hunt you down
  • Your house will be raidable (not protected) while you're inside
  • Bounty hunters will be able to use the Dislocator to draw you out from any plot
  • You will not be able to use the bank, inn or anvil
  • You will not be able to drop things, use chests or trading tables
  • You can't access locked guild doors
  • You won't be able to open loot bags of looted players you killed until the timer runs out
